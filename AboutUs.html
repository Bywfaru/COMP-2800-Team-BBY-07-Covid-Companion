Bringing communities together. No privacy
issues. Easy helping process.

Covid Companion allows people to come together and help during this time of crisis when people are living away from their
families due to safety and travel restrictions.Many people are not able to buy/receive some supplies due to various reasons such as
shortage of supplies, money, etc. We help people in getting everday necessities for free just by posting a request of the itme they need.
We help people to donate stuff so that the items can be used wisely. We try to help elderly, students, people with disabilities, people
who are financially struggling as they have lost their jobs due to the shutdown of every business, people who are in isolation/quarantine,
people who have lost someone due to COVID-19. Providing any type of help to the people is our goal. We also take care of charity groups and
hence, the items which will be unclaimed after a specific period of time will go directly to charity groups. In this way, we can hellp them as well
as the items will not be wasted.
