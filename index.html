COVID COMPANION :)

Are you in need of help or able to help others during this pandemic?

We recognize that many people are currently struggling with acquiring basic necessities such as food and personal hygiene products.
Many services have also become inaccessible or increasingly unaffordable. Trustworthy information is also obscured by the increased volume of internet activity.
In times like these, there are many kind-hearted and generous community members that wish to assist others but do not have an organized or centralized platform to offer help.

We want to become that platform to connect communities together through companionship.

Welcome to COVID Companion.
