Need help? Just ask for it!


We know that it is a very difficult time for everyone. People are staying away from their families due to safety reasons and travel restrictions. People have lost their jobs and now are facing financial crisis. Students who working and studying , aiming for paying the rent by themselves and even send money to their homeback are now jobless and pressurized about this whole situation. Working from home or having online classes is not everyone's cup of tea. People are not able to purchase essential items due too financial crisis or shortage of supplies in grocery stores. There is a long line outside of every grocery store due to social distancing which makes it difficult for old citizens and people with disabilities.


If you are one of them who needs any kind of help then don't hesitate to ask for. Come forward and follow these easy steps to get help:

  • Post a request of item/service you need
  • add you contact details so that the donor can contact you
  • If the item needed is urgent, flag that post so that it appears on top of the feed

We provide an opportunity to people to seek help and others to come together and help as much as possible.
