Bringing communities together. No privacy
issues. Easy helping process.


Covid Companion allows communities to come together and support each other during this time of crisis.
Many people are not able to acquire key resources due to various reasons such as shortage of supplies, lack of funds, or risk of infection.
We aim to help people in getting their everyday necessities for free by posting a request for an item or service they need.
We also aim to help those who wish to assist others, as users can choose to donate or offer items and services!
Our top priority is helping the elderly and those with disabilities, people who are struggling financially, and those people who are stuck in isolation/quarantine.
Providing any sort of assistance to these groups is our goal.
Any items which are unclaimed after a specific period of time will go directly to charity groups that are specifically combatting COVID-19.
